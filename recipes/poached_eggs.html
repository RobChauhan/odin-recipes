Poached Eggs

Description

Ingredients
  • Eggs
  • Vinegar
  • Water
Steps
  1. Boil water in a pan. Enough to fully submerge eggs
  2. Add one spoon of vinegar to pan
  3. Bring to a boil
  4. Crack eggs into a bowl, keep each egg in a separate bowl to not mix them
  5. Use a spoon or fork to spin the water/vinegar and create a swirl
  6. While the water is siwrling quickly drop the egg into the swirl
  7. Wait 3 minutes then remove egg with a slotted spoon, and repeat for additional eggs
